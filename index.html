An Interactive Exploration of College Basketball Offenses

Do the best college basketball offenses shoot more, or shoot better? Scroll to find out.

By Owen McFadden, Madhav Pudukottai Krishnaraj, Karthik Sankaran, and Yaqub Mir

↓

Every possession ends in a choice: where to shoot from. Some teams crank up the volume, firing away at every opportunity. Others are patient, hunting only for high-value looks. This is an exploration of that tension, across hundreds of Division I teams.
